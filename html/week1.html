Nature

Overview

Nature encompasses all living and non-living things on Earth, from the smallest microorganisms to the vast landscapes and ecosystems that make up our planet.Nature heals us and helps build connection with our freedom,authenticity and our souls.

Ecosystems

Nature consists of various ecosystems such as forests, oceans, deserts, and grasslands, each with its unique set of flora and fauna.The whole surface of earth is aseries of connected ecosystems.Abiotic factors include plants,animals,and other organisms

Biodiversity

Biodiversity is a critical aspect of nature, representing the variety of life forms on Earth and the intricate interconnections between them.Biodiversity is all the different kinds of life you'll find in one area-the variety of animals,plants,fungi and even microorganisms like bacteria that makeup our natural world

Conservation

Efforts are made worldwide to conserve and protect nature through measures like wildlife sanctuaries, national parks, and sustainable practices.It ensures the sustainable utilisation of life support systems on earth.
